## Getting Started with EduMate

EduMate provides a secure authentication system to protect your assessment data and ensure only you can access your generated content.

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sign Up">
    Visit the EduMate signup page and click on "Create Account" or "Sign Up".
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Information">
    Fill in the registration form with:
    - **Full Name**: Your complete name (e.g., "Professor John Smith")
    - **Email Address**: Your institutional or personal email (e.g., "professor@university.edu")
    - **Password**: A secure password with at least 8 characters
    
    <Note>
      Your password must be at least 8 characters long. Choose a strong, unique password to protect your account.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Registration">
    Click the "Create Account" button to submit your registration.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Upon successful registration, you'll see a confirmation message and be automatically redirected to the login page within 2 seconds.
    
    <Info>
      If an account with your email already exists, you'll receive an error message. Try logging in instead or use a different email address.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing In

Once you have an account, signing in is straightforward:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Login Page">
    Navigate to the EduMate login page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Provide your:
    - **Email**: The email address you used during registration
    - **Password**: Your account password
    
    <Tip>
      Use the eye icon next to the password field to toggle password visibility if you need to verify what you're typing.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Click the "Sign In" button. The system will:
    - Validate your credentials
    - Generate a secure access token
    - Fetch your profile information
    - Redirect you to the dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Login Troubleshooting

If you see an error message:
- **"Incorrect email or password"**: Double-check your email and password for typos
- **Connection errors**: Verify your internet connection and try again
- **Account not found**: Make sure you've completed the signup process

## Resetting Your Password

Forgot your password? No problem.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Password Reset">
    On the login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link located next to the password field.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Email">
    Provide the email address associated with your EduMate account and click "Continue".
    
    <Warning>
      For security reasons, the system won't confirm whether the email exists in our database. If your email is registered, you'll receive reset instructions.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create New Password">
    After the system processes your request:
    - Enter your new password
    - Confirm the new password by entering it again
    - Ensure both entries match
    
    <Note>
      Your new password must be at least 8 characters long, just like during initial signup.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Reset">
    Click "Reset Password" to finalize the change. You'll see a success message confirming your password has been updated.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Use the "Back to Sign In" link to return to the login page and access your account with your new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Security Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Strong Passwords" icon="shield">
    Use a unique password that's at least 8 characters long and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Keep Credentials Private" icon="eye-slash">
    Never share your password with anyone. EduMate staff will never ask for your password.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Sign Out When Done" icon="right-from-bracket">
    Always sign out when using shared or public computers to protect your account.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Update Regularly" icon="rotate">
    Consider updating your password periodically to maintain account security.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Session Management

EduMate uses secure token-based authentication:
- Your session remains active as long as your access token is valid
- You can sign out at any time using the "Sign Out" button in the sidebar
- Signing out will clear your session and require you to log in again

<Tip>
  Your authentication token is stored securely and used for all API requests. This ensures your data remains protected throughout your session.
</Tip>
